Celebrating Excellence at Global Pathways International School Yangon

2023 has been an incredible year for Global Pathways International School Yangon, marked by numerous achievements and milestones.


Academic Achievements

Top Scores: Our students excelled in national examinations, with several ranking in the top 1%.
International Success: Participation in global Olympiads resulted in multiple medals in Mathematics, Science, and English.


Extracurricular Excellence

Sports Wins: Our teams won regional and national competitions, showcasing exceptional talent.
Art and Culture: Students received accolades for their creativity in art exhibitions and cultural performances.


Community Engagement

Environmental Projects: Organized tree-planting drives and clean-up campaigns.
Charity Drives: Raised funds and donations to support local charities.

About Us

Formed in 2022 by Strategy First University and Strategy First Education Group, GPIS is dedicated to helping Myanmar students access international universities.
We focus on programs that prepare students for success in global studies. Come join GPIS to open doors to a brighter future!
