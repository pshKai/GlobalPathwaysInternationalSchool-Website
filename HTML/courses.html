Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Explore our diverse range of educational programs designed to empower students at every stage of their learning journey. From foundational courses to advanced diplomas, we offer tailored paths that support academic excellence and prepare students for global opportunities.

Pre-iGCSE

Pre-IGCSE is the Pre-International General Certificate of Secondary Education, which is a program that prepares the necessary subjects and skills as a stepping-stone to attend the IGCSE program that will prepare the best for the international education path.

iGCSE

IGCSE is an abbreviation form of International General Certificate of Secondary Education. IGCSE is an international university entrance examination based on the English language recognized by the UK. In Myanmar, IGCSE is taught using two curricula: Cambridge International Education (CIE) and Pearson Edexcel.

NCC Level (3)

The NCC Level 3 International Foundation Diploma is a one-year program designed to prepare students who have successfully completed secondary education to advance to higher education. It's particularly tailored for those aiming to pursue undergraduate degree programs in the UK and other international universities. The assessments are conducted by NCC, UK.

GED

GED programme is designated to prepare internationally recognized high school diploma for over-16-year-old students who haven't completed matriculation exam or IGCSE O level.

Our Facilities

Explore the range of state-of-the-art facilities at our campuses. Designed to cater to a diverse set of needs and interests, each facility ensures our students can learn, explore, and excel in a supportive environment. From advanced laboratories to extensive sports complexes, discover how our resources help shape the future of every student.

World Class Library

Our library offers a vast collection of resources across various disciplines to support our students' academic growth. Featuring quiet study areas, group workspaces, and digital access to global databases, our library is a hub of knowledge and innovation, open to students and faculty alike.

Badminton

Our state-of-the-art badminton facilities provide students with the opportunity to engage in physical education and competitive sports. The courts are designed to international standards, ensuring a perfect environment for both training and tournaments, fostering teamwork and physical wellness.

Laboratory

Our modern laboratory is equipped with the latest technologies and tools to support cutting-edge research and practical learning. These facilities are designed to offer hands-on experience in scientific experimentation, enabling students to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios.
